About Me

My name is Innocent Boakye Ababio
“A committed and driven MSc Computer Science Student with a sound understanding of of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning & Deep Learning and utilises sound organisation and planning skills to deliver research and Practical projects within set timeframes and to a high quality standard”

I simply enjoy innovation and computing. I got ‘hooked’ five years ago and since then have increasingly focused on Computer Programming until it is 100% of my business thinking and activities today. I research across application of deep learning models in natural language at the moment, looking to develop novel innovation solutions and frameworks where appropriate.
The aim is to drive Artificial Intelligence through research and practical industry projects individually, with teams, and organizations, in their innovation activity, applying what I have gained in experiences and knowledge, to further develop core innovation and understanding.

How I got to this point I can only say it has been varied, challenging but full of fun and learning and always curious about how best to innovate(come up with new ideas) to solve challenging problems. This has taken me to study in China aside Ghana.
I’ve gained experience in positions within academia and the IT industry, as a research assistant at Hubei University of Technology(HBUT), Wuhan China from December 2018 to date, I have been researching on natural language processing specifically on knowledge graph with Dr. Chen at Hubei University of Technology. I have already written the manuscript (Probabilistic heuristics for Link Prediction based on Graph Attention Mechanism) that will be submitted for my first publication out of the three papers I want to publish before graduation in 2020.
I served as teaching assistant at the University of Cape Coast, Ghana from Sep 1, 2017 to Aug 1, 2018.
